Financial Controller

Do you possess the keen eye of an accountant and a deep understanding of finance? We're seeking a motivated Financial Controller for an expanding consultancy firm specializing in finance and advisory services. Your role involves collaborating with various departments to provide strategic financial guidance and ensure financial integrity. Ready for your next career move? We at Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

What’s expected of you?

  • Prepare and present accurate and timely financial statements, reports, and forecasts
  • Guarantee adherence to both local and international financial regulations and standards
  • Recommend and maintain KPIs for performance monitoring
  • Create, implement, and supervise the company's budget and financial projections
  • Provide suggestions to improve financial performance and identify business opportunities
  • Regularly analyse financial data to identify trends, opportunities, and risks
  • Formulate strategic suggestions based on financial analysis
  • Manage cash flow and maintain financial stability by overseeing liquidity
  • Oversee daily cash transactions and funding needs
  • Supervise the accounting team to ensure the accurate and timely recording of financial transactions
  • Ensure compliance with taxation, audit, and regulatory requirements
  • Establish and uphold internal controls to safeguard company assets and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Identify and address financial risks
  • Guide, mentor, and foster the growth of the finance team
  • Nurture a collaborative and efficient work environment
  • Collaborate with top management to develop financial strategies in line with business objectives
  • Offer insights for decision-making processes and contribute to long-term financial planning
  • Execute and oversee compliance with the organization's information systems platforms, while actively contributing to their strategic management

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • A bachelor's degree in finance, accounting, or a related field
  • Proven proficiency in financial management or controller roles
  • Thorough grasp of financial principles, practices, and regulations
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and decision-making skills
  • Competence in financial software and advanced Excel usage
  • Outstanding communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Strong attention to detail and a steady commitment to precision
  • Effective under pressure and adept at meeting deadlines

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Attractive compensation with performance-based bonuses.
  • Prospects for professional development
  • An energetic and diverse workplace atmosphere

 

If you're the star our client is seeking, don't miss out – send in your application today! We're in the process of selecting the top candidates.
