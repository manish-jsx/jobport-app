Accountant

Embark on an exciting journey with our client, a dynamic accountancy firm devoted to delivering top-notch accounting and financial management solutions. They're on the lookout for a driven and detail-oriented Accountant to be a vital part of their expanding team. Sounds interesting? We at Backfills look forward to hearing from you!

 

What's expected of you?

  • Validate and compile vital documentation for setting up clients
  • Lead clients through the onboarding process, handling queries, and providing essential support
  • Perform company secretarial tasks for clients, ensuring adherence to statutory obligations
  • Supervise duties related to annual returns, share transfers, and the management of corporate records
  • Play a crucial role in VAT compilation processes, ensuring precision and timely completion
  • Stay informed about relevant VAT regulations and updates
  • Oversee accounting functions for a designated client portfolio
  • Ensure rigorous adherence to accounting standards and regulatory guidelines
  • Manage day-to-day financial transactions and reporting for assigned clients

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Be a fully or part qualified Accountant
  • Have a minimum of 3 years’ experience working in a comparable role in Malta
  • Demonstrate a robust comprehension of accounting principles and practices
  • Exhibit exceptional attention to detail and organizational prowess
  • Excellent communication skills in English, both written and verbal
  • Proficiency in accounting software and the MS Office suite is essential
  • Showcase the ability to collaborate seamlessly in a team and work independently when necessary

 

What's in it for you?

  • Attractive salary reflecting experience
  • Support for continuous education and professional development opportunities
  • Inclusive and collaborative workplace environment
  • Prospects for career progression
  • Flexible working hours to foster a healthy work-life balance

 

If you are confident you can fulfil the responsibilities detailed above and would like to join this leading financial services company, we are already interviewing and shortlisting applicants. Don't miss out — apply now!
