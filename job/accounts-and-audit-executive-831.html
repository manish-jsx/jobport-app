Accounts and Audit Executive

Keen to work in a growing and stable company with more than 45 years of experience? Our client, a well-known accountancy and audit firm, also dealing with various matters such as tax and VAT, is seeking to employ Accounts and Audit Executives to join their dynamic team.

What’s expected of you?

  • Assist a diverse portfolio of clients with accounting and audit-related tasks
  • Accurately prepare and maintain accounting documents and records
  • Prepare VAT and tax returns and payroll for clients
  • Assist in the preparation of financial statements and other financial reports
  • Liaise with clients to ensure their requirements are understood and met
  • Assist in the audit process
  • Other ad-hoc tasks that might be assigned


What are you expected to bring?

  • A level standard in Accounting
  • In the process of attaining an Accounting qualification or already qualified
  • Positive approach and strong communication skills.
  • Show a sense of responsibility for work.
  • Ability to work efficiently in a team as well as individually
  • Good working knowledge of MS applications.

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive remuneration package
  • ACCA study leave
  • Salary increase for every ACCA exam successfully completed
  • Reduced working hours during July and August
  • Working with a stable, well-known growing firm
  • Other benefits are offered
