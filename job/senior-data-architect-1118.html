Senior Data Architect

Join our client's team as a Senior Data Architect! In this pivotal role, you'll spearhead the design and management of the enterprise data model and workflows. Your mission? Ensuring that all data solutions designed for end-users adhere to best-in-class data modeling principles, resulting in top-notch, user-friendly, and easily accessible data. Plus, you'll be at the forefront of strategic decision-making, optimizing data management practices across the entire enterprise.

 

What is expected of you?

 

  • Work closely with the business teams, data governance team and project management team to define a data quality strategy for the enterprise.
  • Define data architecture elements including conceptual data models, meta data definition and related data validations
  • Analyse, design, and document workflows and data models to support a robust data architecture.
  • Identify potential root causes for data quality issues, propose solutions, and discuss options and outcomes with relevant data and technical stakeholders.
  • Participate in peer reviews of systems upgrades, workflows, and data flows developed by other members of the Data Management and Business Intelligence team.
  • In liaison with other data management colleagues plan and develop the data architecture in support of upcoming features and capabilities on the roadmap
  • Provide as-needed support in defining technical data requirements and conducting testing for systems and strategic platforms.
     

What are you expected to bring?

  • Degree in Computer Information Systems or similar at MQF Level 6
  • Proficient in database technologies, enterprise data modelling and knowledgeable of the different techniques and standards for delivering high quality data architecture
  • You will also possess a minimum of 5 years work experience in Enterprise Web Applications as well as Database operations and SQL in a Microsoft stack environment.
  • An analytical approach to problem solving
  • Attention to detail and the ability to work well independently or as part of a team
  • Excellent communication skills to articulate complex data concepts to non-technical team members.
  • A friendly and approachable disposition.
     

What's in it for you?

  • Excellent working conditions
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Personal advancement potential
  • Fun team of ambitious people
