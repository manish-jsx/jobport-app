Senior Accountant

Are you an accounting professional seeking a fresh career opportunity? Our client, a prominent insurance company in Malta, is experiencing rapid growth and is now looking to bolster its accounting team by adding an experienced Senior Accountant. With excellent benefits and a vibrant, collaborative work atmosphere, this is an opportunity you don’t want to miss! Apply now, we at Ceek are waiting for your application!

 

What’s expected of you?

  • Assess insurance and general accounting documents, leading to the monthly compilation and evaluation of Management accounts
  • Manage the punctual submission of monthly accounting data from external local and international insurance managers for protected cell insurance operation
  • Participate in financial reporting, contributing to the annual forecasting and budgeting procedures
  • Contribute to the completion of Solvency II and other regulatory returns, as well as the finalization of annual audits
  • Offer guidance and supervision to the accounting team
  • Propose and implement ongoing enhancements to systems and processes for improved efficiency and quality service delivery

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Be a fully qualified accountant or in the final stages of an accounting qualification
  • Have a minimum of 3 years’ experience within the finance function, insurance accounting background is an asset
  • Exhibit proficiency in IT and data management
  • Display flexibility and a keen awareness of deadlines
  • Excellent organisational, problem solving and analytical skills
  • Demonstrate exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively and provide coaching and supervision to team members

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive compensation package
  • Bonus for personally referred business
  • Reduced summer working hours
  • Hybrid and flexible working option
  • Volunteering leave
  • Health and dental insurance coverage
  • Employee Assistance Programme
  • Training sponsorships
  • Staff discounts on personal insurance products

 

Ready to make a move? Apply today - interviews are underway!
