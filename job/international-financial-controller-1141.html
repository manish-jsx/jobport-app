International Financial Controller

Are you ready for an exciting opportunity in the fast-paced iGaming industry? A leading international company is actively seeking a Financial Controller to join their vibrant and expanding team. The chosen candidate will report directly to the International Finance Director! This is your chance to thrive in a dynamic and innovative environment at the forefront of gaming excellence.

 

What is expected of you?

  • Prepare, reconcile, and analyze financial data for international management accounts
  • Collaborate with the US controller to compile consolidated financial statements
  • Lead efforts to enhance and automate month-end processes and accounting operations
  • Compile annual international financial reports adhering to IFRS & US GAAP, alongside local statutory financial reports for all international group entities
  • Coordinate and supervise the preparation of tax returns and compliance reporting
  • Manage cash flow, including subsidiary cash requests and weekly reporting
  • Interface with external auditors to support audits and address audit findings
  • Implement new accounting standards, policies, and procedures
  • Monitor budget performance and conduct variance analysis
  • Oversee and manage the international finance team

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • University Degree in Accounting, ACCA qualification or equivalent
  • At least 5 years of experience in a similar role
  • Audit background is an asset
  • Strong analytical, teamwork, organizational skills, and proven ability to multitask
  • Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office, NetSuite, and GoogleDocs
  • Highly motivated, energetic, and result-oriented person with a great eye for details and commitment to accuracy
  • Excellent command of English, written and verbal
  • Intercultural skills and a positive, can-do attitude

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Health Insurance
  • Competitive salary packages
  • Hybrid working option
  • Professional development support
  • Working in an international company 
  • Other benefits are offered

 

Interviews for this International Financial Controller have already started so don’t let this opportunity slip away! Apply with us at Backfills!
