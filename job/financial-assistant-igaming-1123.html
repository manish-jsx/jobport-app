Financial Assistant - iGaming

Are you a proactive and detail-oriented individual looking for a new adventure? One of our clients, a leading iGaming company in Malta, is seeking to employ a Financial Assistant to join their dynamic team. Whether you already are working in iGaming or want to shift into this industry, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Accurately record all financial transactions, categorize expenses, and maintain organized records for easy tracking and reporting.
  • Perform regular reconciliations to ensure alignment between company records and bank statements, promptly resolving any discrepancies.
  • Assist in organizing financial documentation and preparing reports for senior accountants, facilitating smooth financial processes.
  • Support the preparation and submission of VAT returns, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and deadlines.
  • Utilize Sage accounting software to input financial data accurately, maintaining up-to-date and organized records.
  • Coordinate and manage financial schedules, deadlines, and appointments, ensuring timely completion of tasks and compliance with financial obligations.
  • Depending on capacity and expertise, support other financial tasks such as annual returns and corporate matters to contribute to overall finance operations.


 

What are you expected to bring?

  • In possession or in the process of obtaining an Accounting qualification
  • Previous experience in bookkeeping or accounting roles is advantageous
  • Proficiency in using accounting software, especially Sage
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy in financial record-keeping
  • Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently
  • Knowledge of VAT regulations and experience in preparing VAT returns is a plus
  • Effective communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment

 

What's in it for you?

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Hybrid work option with the flexibility to work remotely and in-office
  • Opportunities for professional development and career advancement within a dynamic and growing company
  • Supportive work environment that values integrity, diligence, and excellence
  • Other benefits are offered
