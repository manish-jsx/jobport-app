Recruitment Associate

Are you passionate about connecting exceptional talent with exciting opportunities? We're seeking a dynamic and motivated Recruitment Associate to join our growing team! If you're ready to play a crucial role in shaping the future of talent acquisition, apply now! We at Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

What's expected of you?

  • Identify, attract and engage candidates to fulfill specific business briefs and requirements
  • Provide support and service to internal delivery functions and departments
  • Screen prospective candidates
  • Develop a relationship with candidates
  • Produce both adverts and job descriptions, and place on social media platforms
  • Provide pre-employment and compliance checks in line with relevant legislation
  • Provide support to ensure that the candidates and clients always receive a professional and comprehensive service
  • Develop an understanding of market rates and conditions within your sector
  • Agree and deliver against candidate service levels
  • Provide full administration support, including database management and LinkedIn
  • Keeping abreast of industry trends and news

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • A minimum level of a Level 5 Diploma  in Human Resources or a related field
  • Previous experience in customer care
  • Excellent communication skills in English both written and spoken
  • Ability to engage with candidates and clients at all levels
  • Be a team player with a desire to learn and be coached
  • Proficient in the use of different social media
  • Ability to work on own initiative and be self-motivated
     

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive salary
  • Exciting career opportunities
  • Vibrant and collaborative work atmosphere
     

Don't let this incredible opportunity pass you by! Interviews are already underway, so act quickly and submit your application to be considered.
