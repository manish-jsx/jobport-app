Senior Network and Security Specialist

Join our renowned client as a Senior Network and Security Specialist! Take on exciting tasks in implementing and managing advanced network and security infrastructure for a leading Maltese Financial Services company. Ready for the challenge? We at Ceek would love to hear from you!

 

What's expected of you?

  • Spearhead the design, planning, implementation, configuration, administration, maintenance, troubleshooting, and optimization of IT systems and its architecture
  • Craft and maintain functional specifications along with comprehensive system documentation.
  • Uncover opportunities to elevate infrastructure and streamline processes through technology, ensuring adherence to security and compliance standards
  • Enhance the underlying infrastructure to achieve peak performance, robust security, and optimal availability
  • Scrutinize systems, suggest information security solutions, and establish/maintain computer network and security systems
  • Perform meticulous disaster recovery plans, contribute to seamless business continuity, and manage scheduled/unscheduled maintenance
  • Cultivate strong connections with suppliers, internal stakeholders, and external customers as well as the Technology team
  • Conduct thorough assessments of IT systems and networks to pinpoint vulnerabilities
  • Handle threats, malfunctions, and attacks
  • Administer firewalls, routers, VPNs, and various security tools
  • Perform other duties as might be required from time to time

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Hold a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • A minimum of 5 years of experience in an ICT role
  • Industry certifications, particularly Microsoft MS-101 and MS-500, would be considered advantageous
  • Exhibit expertise in Office 365, Microsoft Azure, and Microsoft Enterprise Mobility and Security
  • Possess a working understanding of networking, firewalls, Web Filtering Solutions, Mail Gateways, Load Balancers, AWS, and Intrusion Detection/Prevention solutions
  • Demonstrate proficiency with the Linux operating system
  • Strong business orientation with proven knowledge of the IT industry
  • Possess excellent communication skills to facilitate effective interaction with both internal and external stakeholders
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Proficiency in teamwork, multitasking, and meeting deadlines
  • Fluent in the English language

 

What's in it for you?

  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Modern, employee-centric facilities
  • Work in a team-oriented environment
  • The opportunity to make a real impact on the success of the organization
  • Excellent career development opportunities

 

This is an excellent opportunity not to be missed! Reach us out and we’ll give you more information!
