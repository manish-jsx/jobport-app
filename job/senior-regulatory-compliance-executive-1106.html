Senior Regulatory Compliance Executive

Are you a Compliance professional who is eager for a new challenge? We are looking for a Senior Regulatory Compliance Executive for our client, a dynamic and growing international iGaming company in Malta.  The chosen individual will report directly to the Head of Compliance.


What is expected of you?

  • Report to the Head of Compliance to stay updated on evolving regulatory standards, ensuring adherence across emerging and established markets
  • Conduct prompt analysis of new regulatory mandates and communicate them effectively to internal stakeholders
  • Facilitate communication between different departments to address regulatory concerns and provide timely responses to inquiries
  • Oversee the application process for licenses as required
  • Develop and refine company policies to align with regulatory requirements
  • Regularly review and update company policies as needed
  • Handle due diligence requests from external entities efficiently
  • Provide guidance and assistance to the organization during internal audit processes
  • Address regulator inquiries and manage related issues effectively
  • Support the preparation of quarterly and annual compliance reports
  • Assist with various compliance-related tasks as necessary


What are you expected to bring?

  • Possession of a related Degree
  • A minimum of 1-2 years of experience in regulatory compliance is essential
  • Proficient in English with strong reading and writing skills; fluency in Spanish is considered an asset
  • Demonstrated excellent writing abilities
  • Familiarity with essential gaming-related concepts
  • Strong teamwork and relationship-building capabilities
  • Capable of working effectively in a challenging environment and meeting tight deadlines
  • Skilled in organizing and prioritizing tasks efficiently, with the ability to adapt to changing priorities
  • Meticulous attention to detail


What's in it for you?

  • Competitive salary packages
  • Training and development
  • Working in an international company with offices outside Malta
  • Hybrid working option
  • Other benefits are offered
