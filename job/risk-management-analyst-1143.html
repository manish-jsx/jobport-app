Risk Management Analyst

Are you eager to take your risk management career to the next step? Look no further! A leading bank in Malta is seeking to recruit a Risk Management Analyst to join their growing team.

 

What is expected of you?

  • Conduct credit assessments for new and existing credit facilities, including financial analyses and risk recommendations
  • Analyze financial reports and business plans to support the Bank's new business portfolio development
  • Ensure that requests for new credit facilities align with the Bank's risk appetite
  • Stay informed of market-based and industry-specific information and developments, compiling and analyzing relevant data
  • Assist in monitoring the Bank's investment portfolio
  • Contribute to the completion of periodic risk management reports
  • Handle ad hoc requests from Senior Management and external stakeholders
  • Monitor and track changes in the credit ratings of the Bank's risk exposures
  • Support the risk team in fulfilling regulatory risk management requirements
  • Assist in updating and compiling risk management policies, procedures, and related documentation to support proper risk governance
  • Maintain knowledge of the Bank's significant risks
  • Stay current with relevant banking and risk-related regulations
  • Assist in monitoring ICT and security risk management controls

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Possess a professional qualification in Banking, Finance, or related fields
  • Proven track record in a similar or related role. Experience in the banking sector would be considered an asset
  • Strong quantitative and analytical skills
  • Proficient in MS Office applications, especially advanced MS Excel
  • Fluent in written and spoken English
  • Demonstrates a proactive attitude and the ability to work independently
  • Upholds high standards of integrity and professional ethics
  • Familiarity with ICT and security risk management internal controls is a plus

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Attractive remuneration package
  • Hybrid working option
  • Free parking
  • Flexible hours
  • Health Insurance
