Finance Manager

Ready to drive the financial success of a leading group of companies in Malta with more than 50 years of experience? We're seeking a skilled Finance Manager who will be in charge of overseeing and leading the dynamic team of our client. Sounds interesting? We at Backfills look forward to receiving your application!

 

What's expected of you?

  • Lead and manage the Accounts department, fostering excellence and collaboration
  • Mentor and support Accounts Executives for their professional development
  • Prepare comprehensive monthly/quarterly management accounts with accurate profit and loss forecasts
  • Generate various financial reports: profit and loss, VAT returns, cash flow, and bank reconciliations
  • Conduct regular analysis of financial data to provide insights for strategic decision-making
  • Assist external auditors, Board Members, and Management during audits to ensure regulatory compliance
  • Maintain robust internal controls and document accounting processes to mitigate risks
  • Monitor cash balances and forecasts to ensure financial stability
  • Conduct weekly/monthly bank statement reconciliations according to company policies
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to develop annual budgets and contribute to the yearly business plan
  • Provide financial insights and analysis to support budgetary decision-making and strategic planning initiatives

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Be a fully qualified Accountant
  • Proven previous experience in a similar role
  • Be well-organized, with a track record of achieving targets and a keen eye for detail
  • Strong communication skills with the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders at all levels
  • Excellent leadership skills
  • Strong proficiency in financial management software and MS Office Suite
  • Display the ability to drive change, take initiative, and act as required
  • Proficient in work planning and time management

 

What’s in it for you?

  • The opportunity to work with a leading company in Malta.
  • Be a key player in shaping the financial success of high-profile projects
  • Competitive salary
  • A collaborative and dynamic work environment
