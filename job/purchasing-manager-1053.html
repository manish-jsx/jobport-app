Purchasing Manager

Do you thrive in a role that promises continuous growth? If so, this opportunity is tailor-made for you! One of our clients, an international retail company, is looking for a Purchasing Manager to act as a crucial link between the Head Office and the Purchasing Department abroad. Interested? We at Backfills would like to hear from you!

 

What’s expected of you?

  • Assist with the management of a diverse range of products while ensuring strict adherence to quality standards
  • Collaborate with the Purchasing Department in both Malta and abroad to formulate effective purchasing strategies
  • Contribute to the selection process for new suppliers of products
  • Assist in the planning and execution of promotional activities and negotiation processes
  • Continuously define and monitor selling prices to ensure competitiveness
  • Conduct thorough market monitoring and trend analysis for informed decision-making
  • Assist in shaping seasonal assortments to meet evolving market demands
  • Maintain open and effective communication with various departments and the international purchasing team

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • A Degree in Economics/Marketing would be considered as an asset
  • A minimum of 2 years’ experience in a similar role
  • Excellent communications skills, in English and Maltese
  • Knowledge of Italian will be an advantage
  • Proficient user of MS Office tools, Excel in particular
  • Willingness to travel abroad

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive salary
  • Career progression
  • Dynamic and diverse work environment

 

If you're enthusiastic about this role, don't let this opportunity pass!
