Junior Accountant

Keen to experience the best of both worlds? We are recruiting for an internal accounting position with the opportunity to work from the office as well as from the comfort of your own home! Our client, a well-established corporate service provider, is looking for a Junior Accountant to join their dynamic team. If you are seeking a new challenge and would like to work in a flexible environment, this is the right opportunity for you!

 

What is expected from you?

  • Assist in maintaining the company's financial records
  • Perform month-end accounting procedures and reconciliations
  • Participate in group billing and credit control function
  • Manage intercompany invoicing and reconciliations
  • Aid in preparing monthly management accounts and annual financial statements
  • Implement internal controls and contribute to control procedure design
  • Support the Accounts Manager in the overall financial management of the Group
  • Handle general accounting duties and prepare ad-hoc reports when required

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • At least 4 ACCA modules completed or a recognized qualification MQF level 5 in Accounting
  • A minimum of 2 years of experience in an internal accounting role
  • Excellent communication skills both verbal and written
  • Good understanding of the preparation of financial statements
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, especially in Excel
  • Strong team-playing skills
  • The ability to work efficiently in challenging situations and meet demanding deadlines

 

What’s in for you?

  • Attractive salary plus annual performance bonus
  • Hybrid working options
  • Private health insurance
  • Additional study leave and allowance

 

Join our client’s dynamic team as a Junior Accountant and unlock your potential in a thriving environment! Apply with us at Backfills!
