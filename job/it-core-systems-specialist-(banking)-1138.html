IT Core Systems Specialist (Banking)

Our client is seeking a motivated IT Core Banking Systems Specialist to join their team. This position entails maintaining and supporting critical core banking applications within a highly regulated environment. Are you ready to take your career to the next step within a leading banking firm? If so, apply now with Backfills!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Maintaining core banking systems, ETL processes, databases, and software updates.
  • Ensuring optimal system performance and availability through monitoring and maintenance.
  • Installing, upgrading, configuring, troubleshooting, and optimizing IT infrastructure.
  • Resolving issues impacting IT operations and liaising with third-party service providers.
  • Monitoring SLAs and ensuring supplier adherence.
  • Testing and maintaining Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery plans.
  • Participating in IT system decisions and implementing cybersecurity policies.
  • Managing IT infrastructure, including hardware, software, networking, and cloud solutions.
  • Promoting best practices and maintaining documentation.
     

What are you expected to bring?

  • Reliable, organized, and team-oriented
  • 3 to 5 years' experience in IT infrastructure support, preferably in financial services
  • Bachelor's degree in IT or equivalent
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms, Microsoft operating systems, virtualization, and cybersecurity
  • Professional certifications are a plus
     

What's in it for you?

  • Competitive remuneration package
  • Hybrid working model
  • Flexible hours
  • Free parking
  • Health insurance
