IT Infrastructure and Security Manager

Join an innovative Financial Services leader! We're on the lookout for an experienced IT Infrastructure and Security Manager to be a key player in our client’s Malta-based team. As part of a specialized group, you'll dive into areas like governance, information systems, software development, security, operations, and service management. Intrigued? We at Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

What's expected of you?

  • Manage a team overseeing network architecture, information security, infrastructure systems, and data centre operations
  • Ensure the team surpasses service level agreements through effective management, maintaining a high level of service
  • Suggest solutions to enhance the Technology infrastructure and security roadmap
  • Ensure projects are completed on time, managing budgets, estimates, and progress reports
  • Cultivate a positive teamwork culture and knowledge-sharing environment
  • Manage the Company’s supplier relationships
  • Perform management tasks, maintaining and updating infrastructure and security applications according to established standards and procedures
  • Manage project financials, including business cases, estimates, budgets, and forecasts
  • Write and record tender documents, conduct technical writing, and perform technical evaluations
  • Generate required management reporting and information
  • Identify and implement strategic solutions for problematic areas.
  • Participate in management meetings, lead task forces, and contribute to special projects as needed
  • Actively engage in working groups and task forces at both local and international levels
  • Perform any other assigned responsibilities as needed

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Hold a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • Possess a minimum of 8 years of experience in ICT, including 4 years in a team management role
  • Demonstrate a solid understanding of the IT industry, with deep knowledge of IT Service Management standards and procedures
  • Excellent communication skills, enabling effective interaction with both internal and external stakeholders
  • Showcase strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Proficiency in teamwork, multitasking, and meeting stringent deadlines
  • Fluent in the English language

 

What's in it for you?

  • Excellent working conditions
  • Over-average pay-grade
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Personal advancement potential
  • Fun team of ambitious people

 

If you have the skills and experience listed above, we'd love to hear from you. We are already recruiting for this role and our client starts interviews next week, so don't miss your chance to apply!
