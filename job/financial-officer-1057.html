Financial Officer

Are you looking for a change and want to be part of a dynamic group of companies in Malta?  We are looking for a Financial officer who has gained some experience in accounting in Malta.  Do not miss out, we at Ceek would love to hear from you!
 

What is expected of you?
  • Conduct reconciliations and maintain accounting records
  • Maintain ledgers and keep records up to date
  • Assist in preparation of reports
  • Assist in the preparation of the monthly management accounts
  • Perform general accounting duties

What are you expected to bring?
  • A minimum of 2 years’ experience within a similar role
  • Be fluent in English
  • Be organised and able to meet deadlines
  • Meticulous and number oriented

What is in it for you?
  • A competitive remuneration package
  • Professional working environment
  • Career progression
