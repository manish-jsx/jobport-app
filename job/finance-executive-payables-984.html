Finance Executive Payables

Are you keen to be part of a stable and well-known group of companies providing various services ranging from security to training? Are you looking for an opportunity to work from the comfort of your own home as well as a dynamic working environment? We are looking for a meticulous Finance Executive - Payables to join our client’s dynamic and growing finance team.



What is expected of you?

  • Record expenditures in the system and ensure accurate reconciliation of creditor balances
  • Assist with end-of-month closing procedures
  • Thoroughly validate received information before making postings
  • Take charge of researching, tracking, and resolving accounting or documentation issues and discrepancies, or appropriately directing them to the relevant parties
  • Maintain consistent communication with various departments to acquire and convey information, as well as rectify transactions
  • Prepare accruals and prepayments sheets for monthly account preparations
  • Post and process journal entries
  • Review and manage the accounts payable inbox daily, promptly addressing emails and escalating matters as needed
  • Process manual and international payments in the banking system
  • Accurately allocate accounts payable supplier payments to the corresponding supplier accounts within the finance system
  • Organize filing systems, including payable files and miscellaneous documentation
  • Perform additional responsibilities as assigned by the CFO or authorized personnel
  • Adhere to all company-established procedures and protocols


What are you expected to bring?

  • Previous experience in a similar role
  • Ability to take the lead and act on what is required
  • Proficient in written and spoken English
  • Team-oriented and able to work with deadlines
  • Work planning and time management skills
  • Strong sense of leadership and learning orientation
  • Good analytical skills
  • Accuracy and attention to detail


What’s in it for you?

  • Excellent career prospects
  • Hybrid working option
  • A competitive salary package
  • Other benefits are offered

 

Ready to expand your horizons? Look no further…  Don’t miss out on this new adventure and apply with us!
