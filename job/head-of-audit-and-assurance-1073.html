Head of Audit and Assurance

Are you looking to progress in your finance career? We are searching for a Head of Audit and Assurance to join a fast-growing, multicultural and reputable CSP that provides audit and advisory services to both local and international clients. In this role, you will be responsible for leading and overseeing a team of audit professionals. Do you have the right expertise? Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Develop, implement, and maintain an annual master audit planner
  • Strategize audits and reviews, and approve audit reports
  • Handle client expectations and gather client feedback
  • Recognize resource needs and distribute resources accordingly
  • Identify, prioritize, and emphasize pertinent risk areas when formulating an audit plan
  • Thoroughly review audit files
  • Prioritize diverse deadlines and oversee client briefs effectively
  • Come up with different testing

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Hold a valid Auditor's warrant issued by the Malta Accountancy Board
  • A minimum of 5 years of previous experience as an auditor
  • At least 3 years’ experience in a Senior position, leading a finance team
  • Demonstrate proficiency in relevant audit ISAs and a confident application of them
  • Oversee the team’s performance, and identify the strengths and weaknesses of each team member
  • Strong interpersonal and leadership skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication abilities
  • Be IT savvy

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive salary
  • Flexible working conditions
  • Health insurance
  • Other attractive benefits
  • Professional and dynamic working environment
