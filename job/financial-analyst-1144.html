Financial Analyst

A well-established company, within the manufacturing sector, is seeking to employ a Financial Analyst to provide day-to-day financial support. If you consider yourself a number cruncher and have an excellent eye for detail, we at Backfills would like to hear from you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Assist with month-end reporting, budget preparation, and ad hoc reports
  • Manage asset registers and stock control reports
  • Oversee the standard costing model for accurate cost and sales reporting
  • Monitor and report factory variances as needed
  • Ensure compliance with finance guidelines and controls
  • Support preparation of profit and loss, balance sheet, and cash flow statements
  • Ensure accuracy of the Nominal Ledger and reconcile major accounts
  • Prepare VAT returns

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Part-qualified Accountant or equivalent qualification 
  • Previous work experience in a similar sector will be considered an asset
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Cost Accounting knowledge and experience
  • Analytical and financial modeling skills
  • Attention to detail

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive remuneration package
  • Hybrid working option
  • Medical Health Insurance
  • Excellent career prospects
  • Other benefits are offered

 

Looking for a new adventure? Don't wait any longer! Interviews for this Financial Analyst role have already started... apply with us at Backfills!
