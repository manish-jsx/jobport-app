Senior Marketing Executive

Ready to revolutionize marketing strategies in a fast-paced distribution environment? Our client is looking for a Senior Marketing Executive to join their team to propel the company towards new heights of success. If this sounds like the perfect match for you, Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Develop and implement comprehensive marketing strategies encompassing both digital and traditional advertising, while aiming to achieve and surpass company goals

  • Carry out a combination of short-term marketing tasks and longer-term marketing strategies

  • Coordinate marketing endeavors while evaluating and reporting campaign performance relative to objectives

  • Collaborate closely with third-party agencies, influencers, suppliers, and internal colleagues to ensure a cohesive approach to marketing campaigns

  • Monitor the marketing budget and ensure optimal resource utilization

  • Observe market trends and analyze consumer activities to pinpoint opportunities

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Proven experience as a Marketing Executive or in a similar role

  • A bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communication, or a related field would be an asset

  • Excellent command of the English language, both written and verbal

  • Proven experience in brand development

  • Experience and skills in developing marketing plans 

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Continuous learning opportunities

  • Work with a dynamic team

  • Career progression
