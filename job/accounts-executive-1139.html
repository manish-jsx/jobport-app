Accounts Executive

Are you a passionate finance professional seeking a great job opportunity to step up your career? Wait no more! A well-established group of companies is seeking to recruit a proactive and professional Accounts Executive. If you consider yourself a number cruncher and have an excellent eye for detail, don't wait any longer!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Prepare management accounts
  • Help with yearly budget planning
  • Put together tax and VAT returns
  • Update the list of fixed assets
  • Create reports for management and regulatory bodies when needed
  • Follow company policies and procedures
  • Build connections with other departments on financial issues
  • Prepare financial documents for internal audits and support external auditors
  • Assist in understanding and implementing suggestions from audits

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Qualified Accountant or near completion of an Accounting qualification
  • Have at least 3 years of relevant experience in a similar role
  • Up-to-date with best practices in financial management and reporting
  • Proficient in MS Office applications, especially Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Capable of consistently delivering accurate and timely information
  • Possesses a high level of integrity and works effectively within a team

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive remuneration package
  • Working with a growing group with more than 50 years of local experience in different sectors
  • Discounted parking
  • Flexibility
  • Health insurance
  • Study leave
  • Discount card across the Group
  • Excellent career prospects
  • Other benefits are offered
