Operations Officer

Are you a dynamic and self-motivated individual looking to join a leading international insurance company? We’re looking for an enthusiastic and hard-working individual to join our client as an Operations Officer. If you are looking for your next career opportunity, we at Backfills would love to hear from you!

 

     What's expected of you?

● Perform market research on maintenance products and conduct competitor benchmarking

● Create and prepare product proposals

● Assist with registering and administering service agreements

● Facilitate initiatives that enhance the customer offering whilst achieving operational efficiencies

● Address issues raised by the company’s European market

● Issue invoices and process sales requests

● Provide daily assistance to the Head of Operations and the Operations Team

 

     What are you expected to bring?

● A degree or equivalent in business, information technology, engineering, or mathematics

● Strong interpersonal and communication skills in English, both written and verbal

● Possess a problem-solving mindset

● Exceptional organisational skills and attention to detail

● Ability to prioritise tasks and adapt to changing circumstances

● Proficient in Microsoft Office (in particular Excel) and generating forms using Adobe

● Programming / IT knowledge will be considered a strong asset.

 

     What’s in it for you?

● Medical insurance after probation

● Parking

● Career prospects

● Hybrid work option

● Flexible hours

 

If you think you're the perfect fit for this role, don't hesitate to reach out - our client is already starting interviews next week!
