Marketing Executive

Are you an experienced Marketing or Digital Marketing professional looking for an exciting new job adventure? We are looking for a Marketing Executive to join the dynamic Commercial team of one of the most stable and renowned companies in Malta. If this sounds like you, we at Backfills would like to hear from you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Manage, plan and implement comprehensive marketing initiatives, from SEO to CRM, database marketing, social media, signage, and traditional marketing channels
  • Formulate marketing and communication strategies both for individual products and advertising campaign by collaborating with product owners
  • Prepare agency briefs as needed
  • Evaluate and report on the effectiveness of all marketing campaigns
  • Research and identify trends and insights, conduct tests such as A/B Tests to maximize spend and marketing ROI
  • Serve as the primary liaison between the Company and its marketing agencies and suppliers
  • Oversee the Company’s social media presence and manage its website’s content
  • Organize all the media assets, including images and videos
  • Formulate and monitor the marketing budget

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • A Degree in Marketing, Digital Marketing or related field
  • A minimum of 3 years’ experience in marketing; digital marketing experience will be preferred
  • Previous experience with testing methods and analytical tools, such as Google Analytics, and with setting up google Adwords and Meta Ads
  • Previous experience in managing SEO, CRM and database marketing, social media and display advertising campaigns
  • Strong analytical and data skills, with the ability to identify and stay abreast of the latest digital marketing trends and practices
  • Excellent communication skills in the English language

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive remuneration package
  • Career development opportunities
  • Work in a fast-paced environment

 

If this opportunity intrigues you, don’t miss it out and apply now!
