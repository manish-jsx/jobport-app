Sales Administrator

Our client, a leading distribution company based in Malta, is looking for an enthusiastic Sales Administrator to join their growing team. If you are eager to join a dynamic sales team, and think you have what it takes, apply now with Backfills!
 

What's expected of you?

 

  • Effectively respond to email and call enquiries

  • Prepare and present statistical reports

  • Generate sales memos and sales trade proposals

  • Maintain and update the company's internal product catalogue

  • Process suppliers' purchase orders and create invoices 

  • Record stock on the computer system 

  • Provide administrative support to sales team

 

What are you expected to bring?

 

  • O level standard of education

  • Excellent written and verbal communication in English

  • Proficient with numerical data

  • Strong coordination and organizational abilities

  • Proven Microsoft skills, especially within Excel

  • Capable of working in a fast paced environment whilst meeting deadlines

  • Ability to work both in a team and independently


 

What’s in it for you?

 

  • Career progression

  • Work with a dynamic team

  • Continuous learning opportunities 

 

If you're organised and enjoy a fast environment, this could be your perfect opportunity!
