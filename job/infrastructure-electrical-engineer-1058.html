Infrastructure Electrical Engineer

Are you an Electrical Engineer seeking a fresh career opportunity? Our client, a highly regarded and well-established company in Malta, is actively seeking the ideal candidate to join the Infrastructure Department team. The selected individual will serve as the key representative of the Engineering department in the field of electrical engineering. Intrigued? We at Backfills are eager to connect with you!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Provide technical support and guidance to management and workshop staff on matters related to the organizational structures and facilities, needed for the operation of the enterprise
  • Maintain an ongoing improvement initiative and oversee quality control for the Electrical Engineer
  • Actively engage in projects and take the lead on assigned projects
  • Identify technical and operational solutions to help achieve the engineering department's goals
  • Prepare technical reports to facilitate informed decision-making by management
  • Assist the technical department in planning and executing campaigns
  • Collaborate closely with the workshop manager to refine maintenance plans and enhance reliability
  • Organisation of repairs and maintenance of company assets, installations, infrastructures, equipment and tools, and buildings
  • Undergoing training from manufacturers, disseminating information, and spearheading personnel technical development
  • Participate in the annual budget and forecast for the workshop
  • Plan the allocation of necessary human and material resources
  • Follow-up on certification on all premises permits, plans and electrical diagrams
  • Ensure adherence to policies concerning security, data protection, health and safety, quality assurance, and environmental protection
  • Perform any other task as might be requested by the management

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Strong organizational skills with the flexibility to adapt to a dynamic environment
  • Proficiency in preventive maintenance techniques
  • Ability to collaborate effectively in a team and foster teamwork
  • Capable of offering guidance to colleagues on technical matters
  • Demonstrated leadership and management capabilities
  • Exceptional analytical skills

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Work with a well-known and stable company
  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Career progression opportunities
