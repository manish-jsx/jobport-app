Management Accountant

Do you want to be a part of a professional and dynamic team? Do you consider yourself to be a hands-on individual? If so, one of our clients, a leading company in the security business with more than a century of expertise, is looking to employ a Management Accountant. The chosen individual will be reporting directly to the CFO!

 

What is expected of you?

  • Ensure punctual and precise accounting procedures
  • Posting journal entries
  • Managing payment processes
  • Conducting reconciliations for bank, stock, payroll, and billing
  • Executing month-end closing tasks
  • Compiling management accounts
  • Computing quarterly VAT and submitting returns
  • Supporting annual financial audits and coordinating with external auditors
  • Collaborating with banks
  • Developing and maintaining finance protocols
  • Upholding compliance with internal controls
  • Aiding in monthly data submissions requested by the CFO
  • Fulfilling additional responsibilities as directed by the CFO



What are you expected to bring?

  • 3 years of experience in a similar position
  • Completed or in the final stages of completing ACCA or a similar qualification
  • Exceptional attention to detail
  • Capable of working effectively under pressure and meeting tight deadlines
  • Proficient in multitasking
  • Demonstrates strong planning and organizational abilities
  • A team player with excellent initiative
  • Displays a positive and proactive attitude

 

What's in it for you?

  • Dynamic and stable work environment
  • Flexi-time is offered
  • Hybrid option after training
  • Competitive salary package
  • Other benefits are offered
