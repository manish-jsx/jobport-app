Assistant Financial Controller

Are you an analytical individual on the lookout for a new opportunity? If so, we are seeking to recruit an Assistant Financial Controller for one of our clients, a prominent figure in the hospitality industry, that offers amazing benefits and perks. The chosen candidate will have the opportunity to become a Financial Controller!

 

What’s expected of you?

  • Collaborate in preparing comprehensive monthly management accounts, inclusive of various end-of-month reports
  • Support cash flow management activities, ensuring optimal liquidity positions and prudent financial decision-making
  • Implement robust quality control measures over financial transactions and reporting
  • Contribute to the timely submission of tax, compliance, and other regulatory returns and questionnaires
  • Facilitate and maintain relationships with financial institutions, investors, and stakeholders
  • Assist in the annual budgeting process, aligning financial goals with organizational objectives and providing valuable insights for strategic planning
  • Prepare requisite documentation for external audits, aiding the Financial Controller in liaising with auditors
  • Support the consolidation of yearly financial reporting for the Company
  • Drive continuous improvement initiatives by identifying opportunities to enhance efficiency and strengthen internal controls
  • Provide guidance and mentorship to the finance team, fostering professional development and conducting performance evaluations to optimize team performance
  • Aid in compiling VAT reports and Eco tax submissions
  • Address day-to-day queries from management
  • Offer constructive feedback to the Financial Controller, contributing to ongoing refinement of financial processes and strategies
  • Foster collaboration within the finance team and across departments
  • Remain adaptable and responsive to evolving business needs, undertaking any additional tasks within the purview of the finance team to support the smooth operation of the business

 

What are you expected to bring?

  • Be a part or fully qualified Accountant
  • Previous experience in a similar role
  • Demonstrated commitment to high work ethics and integrity in financial practices
  • Profound comprehension of accounting principles, financial regulations, taxation legislations, and financial reporting standard
  • Sound knowledge of financial planning, budgeting, and analysis methodologies
  • Hands-on experience in cash flow management, cost control, and financial risk assessment strategies
  • Exceptional leadership skills coupled with strong interpersonal abilities, enabling effective collaboration and influence across diverse stakeholders
  • Proficiency in financial software and systems, coupled with advanced Excel skills

 

What’s in it for you?

  • Competitive salary
  • The opportunity to become a Financial Controller
  • Flexi-hours
  • Company discounts
  • Parking
  • Opportunity to join a dynamic team
  • Other benefits are offered

 

If you are looking for the next step in your accounting career, this is the perfect opportunity for you! Interviews for this Assistant Financial Controller role have already started so... what are you waiting for?! Apply with us at Backfills!
